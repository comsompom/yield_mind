The Problem

Users do not know where to deploy for yield

  • DeFi users struggle to compare APY, risk, and execution complexity.
  • Bridging and transaction steps are fragmented across tools.
  • Most users cannot convert strategy intent into safe actions quickly.

Impact: opportunity loss, execution mistakes, and low retention.

The Solution

YieldMind: AI assistant for portfolio actions

Analyze

Read wallet context + opportunities in a single dashboard.

Recommend

Natural language prompts produce actionable strategy suggestions.

Execute

One-click action flow connected to wallet transaction handling.

Bridge

Move assets into Initia without leaving the product workflow.
